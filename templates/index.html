  1. Aim To win the most points by accurately predicting how many tricks you'll get in each round
  2. The dealer takes a card from remaining deck and place it in the middle.
  3. Players begin to bid how many tricks they can take.
  4. Play begins: you must put same color on top of trump.
  5. The hand will be won by the highest card.
  6. If you do not have the color, you can place your trump down or place place down any card.
  7. Once all cards have been played, the dealer tallies up the score.
  8. If a player takes the exact number of tricks he bid, he receives 10 points, plus the number he bid.
  9. If a player takes any other number of tricks, he receives no points.
